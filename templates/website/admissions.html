Admissions

How to Apply

  1. Step 1: Purchase Application Form

    Visit the school administrative office to obtain the admission form for your child.

  2. Step 2: Submit Documents

    Return the completed form with all required documents (Birth Certificate, previous results, passport photographs, etc.).

  3. Step 3: Entrance Examination

    Your child will sit for an entrance examination on the scheduled date to assess readiness for admission.

  4. Step 4: Interview & Admission

    Successful candidates are invited for an interview, after which admission letters are issued.
